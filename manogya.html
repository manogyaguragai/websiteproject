Gaming is playing an electronic video game, which is often done on a dedicated gaming console, PC or smartphone. People who often play video games are called gamers.
Gamers are often ordered into several groups, depending on how often and how involved they are with gaming. A casual gamer is someone who irregularly plays games or who only plays a single low-intensity game. A hardcore gamer is someone who regularly plays video games. A professional gamer is someone who makes money or supports themselves by playing games.
